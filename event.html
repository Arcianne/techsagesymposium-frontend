Learn Modern Web Development

with Mika Soriano

@Kaisa Hall, Tarlac City

January 05, 2025 at 10:00 AM

Join Mika Soriano in this comprehensive seminar designed to equip you with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in modern web development. This session will cover essential topics such as responsive design, building interactive user interfaces with frameworks like React and Vue.js, and understanding backend development with Node.js. Whether you're a beginner looking to enter the field or a developer seeking to upgrade your skills, this seminar offers practical, hands-on learning to help you master both frontend and backend web development.

About the Speaker

Mika Soriano

Full-Stack Web Developer & Educator

Mika Soriano is a full-stack web developer with over a decade of experience building dynamic, high-performance websites and applications. She has worked with global companies such as CodeSphere and DevEdge Solutions, where she led teams in developing cutting-edge web solutions using modern frameworks like React, Vue.js, and Node.js. Mika is passionate about teaching the next generation of developers and has been a featured speaker at numerous web development seminars and bootcamps. She specializes in breaking down complex topics such as responsive design, API integration, and server-side rendering.
