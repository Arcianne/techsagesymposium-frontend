About Us

TechSageSymposium is dedicated to connecting everyone that is interested in learning and teaching in technology through seminars and webinars. We want to empower individuals with the latest knowledge and skills in the ever-evolving tech landscape. It is relevant and useful for everyone that wants to be informed about the available seminars that they could attend. It also provides a valuable resource for anyone looking to enhance their skills and knowledge in technology.

TechSageSymposium began as a collaborative project by Information Technology students from Tarlac State University.

Launched on September 29, 2024, it combines our skills and creativity to serve a meaningful purpose: providing access to seminars and webinars that connect our community.

TechSageSymposium is for those who are passionate about technology and eager to grow. It's where innovators, learners, and professionals come together to master the latest tech trends, connect with industry leaders, and unlock new career opportunities.

Meet Our Team

MISSION

Our goal is to provide dynamic platform for everyone to access the expert-level seminars and webinars to further develop the skills and to share the knowledge by offering a simple and easy-to-use platform that unites professionals, enthusiasts, and through leaders with the newest ideas and information. Our objective is to make it easier for a wide range of people to find, join up for, and attend excellent events.

VISION

Our aim is to become the primary digital center for conferences and meetings, supporting an informed and connected community to foster advancement, cooperation, and knowledge exchange among numerous fields and professionals.
